About

BITSHack is the annual hackathon organized by BITS Pilani Goa. Our mission is to bring the community together to creat innovative solutions to novel problems. We want to creat a platform to bring together minds working at the cutting edge of new technologies. Our focus this time is on some of the latest upcoming technologies.
