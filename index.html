FAQ

Everyone is welcome to apply, be it students, professionals or turing-test certified androids. If you are under 18 years of age, we’ll need a parental consent form.

The application period will close on at 11:59 pm (IST). We evaluate applications on a rolling basis and acceptances will be sent every weekend via email. Once accepted, you’ll have three days to confirm your attendance.

We’re looking for people who "do"! Folks who are passionate enough to work on crazy world-changing ideas. Tell us what makes you apply to BITSHack, what excites you, accomplishments you are proud of and whatever else you think can strengthen your chances of acceptance. We’ll get to know more about your abilities from the past projects, GitHub profile, participation/awards in other hackathons.

Zero. Zip. Zilch. Nada. Nothing. Admission to ETHIndia is completely free, thanks to our sponsors! We'll provide you with a weekend's worth of schwag, meals, drinks, snacks and a place to crash when you need a break from coding.

Yes! This hackathon is strictly a team competition. We believe that you’re stronger as a team than you are apart. You can join or create a team once you've started an individual application.

You can form teams of up to 3 people. Most teams aim to have a mix of people with both design and developer skills.

Not to worry, most people don’t! We’ll have team formation and ideation events geared towards helping you find people to work with.

Weapons of any kind, drugs, or alcohol. If you’re not sure whether something will be okay, please ask ahead of time!
